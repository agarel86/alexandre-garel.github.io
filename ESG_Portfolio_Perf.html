Methodology

Portfolio Construction

Value-weighted (market-cap weighted, in EUR). Formation year Y → uses ESG data as of end-of-year Y−1 to screen stocks. Market capitalisation in EUR as of end-of-year Y−1 determines each stock’s weight. Benchmark = all constituents with returns and market cap data. Portfolio = benchmark minus excluded stocks. Monthly portfolio return = Σ(wi × ri) where wi = MCapi / Σ MCap. Stocks without market cap data for the weighting year are excluded from both benchmark and portfolio. Note: these results are gross of fees — a real fund implementing any active screening strategy would charge management fees that would reduce the reported returns.
