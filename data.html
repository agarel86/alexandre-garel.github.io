Open Access Datasets

Data

The following datasets are made publicly available to support open science and reproducibility in empirical finance research. They accompany peer-reviewed publications and may be freely used with appropriate citation.

Open Access · Industry-Country Level
Corporate Biodiversity Footprint
Mean CBF from IDL at the industry-country level. Based on an international sample of 2,106 listed firms from 34 countries over the years 2018–2021.
Source: Garel, A., Romec, A., Sautner, Z., & Wagner, A. F. (2024). Do investors care about biodiversity? Review of Finance, 28(4), 1151–1186.
Download (.xlsx)
Open Access · Firm Level · US
Employee Demographic Diversity
Employee demographic diversity at the firm level for a sample of 2,269 unique US firms over 2000–2021, covering age, gender, and ethnicity dimensions.
Source: Frijns, B., Garel, A., & Liao, S. (2024). Employee demographic diversity and firm performance. Financial Management. Forthcoming.
Download (OSF)
Open Access · Global · 117 Countries
Firm-Level Nature Dependence
Measures of nature dependence to ENCORE ecosystem services for 31,772 listed firms from 117 countries between 2010 and 2023. Includes both granular ecosystem service scores and an overall NatureDep index.
Source: Garel, A., Romec, A., Sautner, Z., & Wagner, A. F. (2025). Firm-level nature dependence. Review of Finance. Forthcoming.
Download (OSF)
